ABOUT

Hello!

I am a first-generation Canadian-born Chinese user experience and user interface (UX/UI) designer. My passion lies in user research: personas, ethnography, user interviews, etc. As someone who enjoys personal understanding and reflection, user research allows me not only to exert empathy and learn about individuals, but to learn more about myself in the process. As a third culture child, I strive to gain a better understanding of myself as both ethnically Chinese and a Canadian citizen while fitting in Western society as a designer.

Projects

Feature Design: Trello Instant Messaging

In a team of two, studied Trello to understand its language such as conventions, function, and interactions. Through user interviews, learned that users often communicate with a different application and developed an additional feature within Trello to keep users within the application.

View Project

User Research: Surrey Fire Hall 11

In a team of two, worked alongside the City of Surrey Fire Fall 11 to exercise participatory design. Through interactive research design methods, found that their existing Accountability Board used for fire calls provides increased stress to fire captains and battalion chiefs. Developed a digital application of the Accountability Board for better streamlining.
